CAIQ v4.1 · Question IAM-02 · SCF:IAC-06 · domain: Identity and Access Management

Are users required to use multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all administrative access to systems?

Expected answer type: yes / no / n.a. · Auditor inspection: spot-check

confidence 0.91
Recommended answer: Yes

Yes. Sentinel Labs enforces phishing-resistant multi-factor authentication (WebAuthn preferred, TOTP fallback) for all administrative access to production and corporate systems. Enforcement is implemented via Okta workforce policy and audited continuously through the security-atlas evidence pipeline. AWS console and CLI access additionally require MFA per IAM password policy. Coverage at the time of this response: 94% of administrative users (98% production, 100% prod-eu, 92% contractor workspace; 2 contractor accounts in remediation, tracked under EXC-007).
